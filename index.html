Why EGS.

EGS is comprised of advisory and technical teams with years of corporate, field, and consulting experience––and who are skilled at information security consulting. Our accomplished team allows EGS to demonstrate a vast knowledge of industry standards, benchmarks, and best practices that assure the best solution is offered to our clients. Each consultant is a noted and published expert in his or her respective fields. Information security and operational risk consulting and advisory is the sole focus of our practice; it is not the sideline interest of a general consulting company.

Additionally, EGS assigns an onsite team of InfoSec professionals and can make available a much broader team of exceptionally credentialed operational risk and resiliency experts in the fields of business continuity management, IT disaster recovery, emergency and crisis management, cyber-security forensics, and crisis media management strategy (if required).

Even more broadly, EGS is dedicated to helping organizations protect and enhance enterprise value in an increasingly complex legal, regulatory, and economic environment––with consulting professionals helping anticipate, illuminate, and overcome complex business challenges.
